How installation works

The clinic owner does not build anything. The creator manages the setup.

👨 Creator does this

  • Collect clinic details
  • Change demo to clinic branding
  • Add chat button to website
  • Connect AI replies and lead form
  • Test everything
  • Go live

👩 Business owner does this

  • Sends logo, services, hours and phone number
  • Gives website access or developer contact
  • Approves the final setup
  • Receives leads and follows up
