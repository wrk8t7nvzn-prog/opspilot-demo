How installation works

The clinic owner does not build anything. The creator manages the setup.

Creator’s job

  • Collect clinic logo, services, phone, website, booking link.
  • Change the demo from “Beauty Med London” to the real clinic name.
  • Add the AI chat button to the clinic website.
  • Connect leads to email, Google Sheet, or dashboard.
  • Test it, then go live.

Business owner’s job

  • Send business details.
  • Give website access or introduce website person.
  • Test the demo.
  • Approve it.
  • Use the leads.
